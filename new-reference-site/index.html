Meet Harper, Your Hero!

Harper is a spirited red panda with boundless energy and an unshakeable determination to protect his home. When the malevolent spirit Kreakli emerges from the Dark Oak Tree, Harper must master incredible abilities and explore vibrant worlds to save his friends and restore peace to the festival.

Kreakli's Demise

Awakened from the gnarled heart of the Dark Oak Tree, Kreakli is a malevolent spirit trapped within the pale moonstone. Once dormant, he now spreads chaos and destruction throughout the peaceful festival grounds. His shadow threatens to consume everything Harper holds dear.

Only by collecting the scattered musical notes, solving ancient puzzles, and mastering transformation abilities can Harper hope to stop Kreakli before it's too late...
